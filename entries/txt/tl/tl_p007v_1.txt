
 For giving the color of all kinds of
 
  metals
 
 to
 
  wood
 
 & other things
 
  Take
  
   touchstone
  
  &
  
   pumice stone
  
  as much of one as
of the other & grind them together & mix with a
  
   hide glue
  
  stronger than that used for painting, & paint it on
whatever you like. Once dry, rub this layer with any
  
   metal
  
  whatsoever, then burnish it.
