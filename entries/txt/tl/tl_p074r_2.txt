
 For making
 
  vermilion
 

  Take two
  
   pounds
  
  of
  
   sulfur
  
  , & melt it, & put
it into two
  
   pounds
  
  of
  

    merecuse
   



    mercury
   

  . And if this substance ignites, cover it quite
gently, so the air does not pass into the
  
   pot
  
  , then put the
whole for some time after into a
  

    leaded
   
   pot
  
  , onto the
fire
  

    for the space of twenty-four hours
   

  . You will
have
  
   good vermilion
  
  .
