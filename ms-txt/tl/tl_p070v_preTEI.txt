










Copper <-is meil-> and latten are proper to
cast & at the right heat when they throw off, while stirring them in the
crucible, a very white flame & that the bath is very
liquid.

Some cast in well ground ceruse <-for
the->, others in flour moistened several times with
oil <-& g-> & dried in the
sun.

Others, for casting in lead, old
printers' type or the composition thereof.

Others put into the tin or lead a substance of
fixed quicksilver which makes it run.
